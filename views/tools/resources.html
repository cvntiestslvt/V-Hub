NCLEX Practice
📝
RegisteredNurseRN
registerednursern.com
Free NCLEX practice questions with detailed rationales. Organized by topic. Better than most paid review centers for understanding the why.
↗ open
🧠
Nurseslabs
nurseslabs.com
Filipino nursing resource. Nursing care plans, NCLEX reviews, free quizzes. Familiar with local school formats. Good for NCP practice and understanding local exam structure.

🎓
NCLEX Mastery — Free Mode
nclexmastery.com
Adaptive difficulty question bank. Focus on priority questions and SATA — most PH board exams now use NCLEX-style formats.

Reference Tools
💊
Drugs.com
drugs.com
Free drug reference. Dosage, interactions, nursing considerations. Use before any pharmacology concept session.
↗ open
🏥
MedlinePlus
medlineplus.gov
NIH patient education resource. Read the simple-language version of a condition before the textbook chapter — it primes your Feynman technique before you hit the detail.
↗ open
Anatomy & Physiology
🧬
OpenStax Anatomy & Physiology
openstax.org/books/anatomy-and-physiology
Free, peer-reviewed A&P textbook. Full substitute for a paid textbook. Download PDF and annotate in Obsidian.
↗ open
🫀
Khan Academy — Health & Medicine
khanacademy.org/science/health-and-medicine
Free A&P videos with MCAT/nursing context. Cardiovascular, renal, respiratory, endocrine. Watch before reading a chapter — builds the "why" first.
↗ open
German
🇩🇪
Nicos Weg — Deutsche Welle
dw.com/en/learn-german/nicos-weg
Free A1–B1 German course from Deutsche Welle. Video-based, native speakers, subtitle support. Start Month 3 in your study plan.

🎙
Goethe Institut — Free Practice
goethe.de/en/spr/ueb.html
Official certification body. Free practice materials for A1–B2. Familiarize yourself with the exam format years before you sit it.

Nursing
▶
RegisteredNurseRN
youtube.com/@RegisteredNurseRN
Best free nursing YouTube channel. NCLEX-style explanations, clinical judgment, med-surg basics. Covers nearly every nursing school topic. Start here for any concept you can't visualize from text alone.
↗ open
▶
Simple Nursing
youtube.com/@simplenursing
Memory tricks, mnemonics, simplified explanations for complex topics. Good for pharmacology and pathophysiology. High novelty — good for low-energy study days.
↗ open
▶
Dirty Medicine
youtube.com/@dirtymedicine
Medical and pharmacology content explained fast without fluff. Useful for understanding how drugs work rather than memorizing classes.
↗ open
A&P
▶
Professor Dave Explains
youtube.com/@ProfessorDaveExplains
The best free A&P on YouTube. Cellular biology, organ systems, biochemistry. Clear, accurate, appropriately deep. Watch before reading a chapter, not after.
↗ open
▶
Armando Hasudungan
youtube.com/@armandohasudungan
Hand-drawn illustrated explanations of pathophysiology and anatomy. Uniquely good for visual learners. Cardiology, renal, hematology. Slower pace — good for consolidation sessions.
↗ open
German
▶
Easy German
youtube.com/@EasyGerman
Native speakers in real situations with German + English subtitles. Best exposure content available. Watch one video per week from Month 1. Trains your ear before any grammar study begins.
