Low Energy Protocol
Diagnose the type first. Apply only that level's intervention. Wrong diagnosis = wrong fix = still not studying.

0
Mild resistance — "I don't feel like it"
You can move, just avoiding. Intervention: open the physical notebook and write the concept name at the top. That's the only task. Resistance dissolves once you've written one word.
1
Overwhelm — "there's too much, I don't know where to start"
Paralyzed by scope, not laziness. Intervention: open tracker.md and read your ✓'s. Then pick the smallest possible unit — "read the first paragraph of one section." That's the whole task.
2
Brain fog — "I'm staring at the page but nothing is going in"
Brain is offline, not broken. Intervention: stop studying. Walk outside 10 minutes — no phone. Come back. German 15 minutes only. If brain is back, do one concept. If not, reading block and sleep.
3
Full shutdown — emotional event, financial stress, complete crash
Burnout day, not a lazy day. Intervention: German 15 minutes. Read your current book. That's it. Do not guilt yourself into studying through a shutdown — worse retention than not studying, and you'll associate studying with suffering.
The goal on a bad day is not productivity. It's not breaking the chain. German 15 min keeps the chain alive at every level. Everything else is optional.

Novelty
Boredom disguised as avoidance
Find the clinical horror story first. "Medication error [drug class]" before opening the textbook.
Perfectionism
Can't start because it won't be perfect
Deliberately make a messy note. Ugly, incomplete, wrong structure. Done beats perfect every single time.
Emotional
Stress or anxiety blocking access
Write one sentence about what's actually bothering you in Daily/. Named anxiety has less power than ambient anxiety.
Fake work
Busy but not actually studying
Test: did you produce an Obsidian concept note today? No = haven't studied. Stop reorganizing. Write one concept name.
Viktoria-specific traps — recognize them by name
TrapRedesigning the system instead of using it
FixWrite the system idea in "System ideas — open day 30." Close Obsidian. Open the physical notebook. Write one concept name.
TrapThe rabbit hole — one lookup, now 6 tabs open
FixWrite the lookup question in the notebook margin. Continue the session. Answer it only after the self-test at the end.
TrapThe reading exit — fiction during a study block
FixName it out loud: "This is the reading exit." Then decide consciously. The only wrong move is not naming it.
TrapOne bad day becoming one bad week
FixRe-entry: Day 1 German only → Day 2 German + one synthesis sentence → Day 3 full minimum. Never compensate with a marathon.
